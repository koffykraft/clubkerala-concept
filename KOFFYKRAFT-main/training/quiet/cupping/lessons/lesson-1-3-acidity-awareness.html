Cupping 03

Acidity

Aim

Separate brightness from sourness.

Start

Use two cups; wait until warm, not hot.

Do

  • Taste warm and cooler.
  • Compare sharpness.
  • Write one familiar reference.

Notice

  • Bright
  • sour
  • sharp
  • fruit-like

Words

Acidity

Use your own food memory: nimbu, mosambi, raw mango, tamarind, kokum, apple, grape. The point is not the correct fruit. Ask whether the acidity feels pleasant, harsh, or dull.
