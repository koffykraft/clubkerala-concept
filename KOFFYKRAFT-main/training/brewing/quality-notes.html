Quality and Beginner-Friendliness Notes

Editorial notes for the revised Brewing course.

Main changes

  • The previous Brewing pages were compressed from a much richer curriculum.
  • This revision preserves the original lesson text and adds a consistent beginner self-study wrapper.
  • Every lesson now foregrounds the brewing loop: brew, taste, diagnose, change one thing, record.
  • The original quizzes, answers, guided exercises, and reflections are retained when present.
  • Recipes are explicitly framed as starting points because grind, water, equipment, coffee age, roast, and taste interact.
  • Espresso, milk, and water lessons include stronger caution against unsafe or overconfident adjustments.

Rule used

Keep the curriculum's substance. Rewrite the page structure so a learner can practice alone, diagnose gently, and build notes without pretending one recipe works for every coffee.
