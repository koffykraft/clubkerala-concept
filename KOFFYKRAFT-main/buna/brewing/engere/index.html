KoffyKraft Brewing

Engere

What it is Coffee leaf brew with milk.

Fresh or mature coffee leaves are brewed in water, strained cleanly, then combined with fresh cow's milk. Household versions may be plain, sweetened, or spiced.

Start 200-250 g fresh leaves, 1 litre water, 400 ml milk.

Boil or simmer the leaves for 7-15 minutes. Strain fully. Add milk while the brew is hot. Serve hot.

Variants Plain, sweetened, spiced.

Sweetened Engere adds honey or sugar. Spiced Engere may include herbs and spices such as rue, coriander, ginger, basil, lemongrass, chilli, cardamom, fennel, and salt.

Boundary Reported tradition, not a medical claim.

The field study records household use and reported experience. It does not prove treatment, lactation, recovery, or performance claims.

Source South Ethiopia household study.

Yohannis, Teka, Tola, and Teferra, 2026. Indigenous coffee leaf brew and Engere brewing practices, Gofa Zone, South Ethiopia.
