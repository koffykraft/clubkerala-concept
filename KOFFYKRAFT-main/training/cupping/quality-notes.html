Revision notes

Quality and Beginner-Friendliness Notes

This page records the editorial improvements made while converting the KoffyKraft notes into a public self-study course.

Main changes

  • The earlier generated pages were too compressed for standalone self-study.
  • This revision keeps the original protocol notes visible, then adds beginner explanation, common mistakes, answer keys, and notebook prompts.
  • Scores are treated as optional until the learner can write clear descriptors.
  • Defects are framed as repeatable clues, not one-sip verdicts.
  • Real-life conditions are acknowledged instead of pretending every learner has laboratory conditions.
  • The repeated learning loop is now explicit on the course home and every lesson: notice, name, compare, record, repeat.

Editorial rule

Preserve the teaching intent of the original notes. Rewrite for clarity. Add scaffolding where a beginner would otherwise be left guessing. Do not make medical, health, or quality claims beyond what the references and repeated tasting can support.
