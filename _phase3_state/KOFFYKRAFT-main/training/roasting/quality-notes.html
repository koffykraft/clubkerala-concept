Revision notes

Quality and Open Reference Notes

Editorial record for making this a serious open learning course.

Main changes

  • Expanded Roasting from a Module 1-only site into a multi-module course covering foundations, applied control, and production/quality systems.
  • Each lesson now builds the student through intention, planning, logging, cupping, brewing, and revision.
  • Cupping and Brewing are explicitly treated as prerequisites and feedback systems.
  • The source notes are preserved inside each lesson, with beginner scaffolding and answer guides added around them.
  • References emphasize SCA, WCR, CQI, Coffee Board of India, and open scientific resources rather than unsupported authority.
  • Copyright boundary: public pages summarize and teach from KoffyKraft notes and public references; they do not reproduce proprietary industry course materials.
