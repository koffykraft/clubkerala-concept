References and Source Notes

External references anchor the course to public industry and scientific resources. Local source files provide the KoffyKraft lesson body.

External references

Specialty Coffee Association - Education

SCA education is a global structured pathway for coffee skills and professional development.

Specialty Coffee Association - Coffee Skills Program

Industry education pathway that includes roasting, brewing, sensory, green coffee, and barista skills.

Specialty Coffee Association - Coffee Value Assessment

Current SCA framework for evaluating coffee through physical, descriptive, affective, and extrinsic assessments.

Specialty Coffee Association - Coffee Taster's Flavor Wheel

Industry sensory vocabulary resource developed with World Coffee Research.

World Coffee Research Sensory Lexicon

Structured sensory vocabulary and calibration reference for coffee aroma and flavor.

Coffee Quality Institute

Coffee quality organization and reference point for quality evaluation and Q programs.

Coffee Board of India

Official Indian coffee sector resource for origin, production, and local context.

Thermal Contaminants in Coffee Induced by Roasting: A Review

Open review discussing roasting-related chemical transformations including Maillard reaction, caramelization, and pyrolysis.
