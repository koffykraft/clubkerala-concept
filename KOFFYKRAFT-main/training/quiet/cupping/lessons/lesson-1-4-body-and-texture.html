Cupping 04

Body and texture

Aim

Notice how coffee feels.

Start

Use the same spoonful size for each cup.

Do

  • Hold the sip.
  • Compare weight.
  • Notice finish.

Notice

  • Thin
  • round
  • creamy
  • dry

Words

Texture

Thin means light or watery. Round means smooth and full. Creamy means soft and coating, not milk. Dry means the mouth feels rough after swallowing.

Record

Question
Observation
Repeatable note
