Cupping 08

Defects

Aim

Recognize obvious faults.

Start

Use small sips; spit if needed.

Do

  • Smell first.
  • Taste carefully.
  • Stop if unpleasant.

Notice

  • Moldy
  • stale
  • fermented
  • burnt

Words

Defect

A defect is a repeated fault, not a taste you personally dislike. Confirm by smelling, tasting, and comparing.

Record

Question
Observation
Repeatable note
Next changeDo not force a bad cup to sound good.
