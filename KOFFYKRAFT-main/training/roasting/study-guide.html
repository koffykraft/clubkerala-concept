Study method

How to Use This Roasting Course

This course is meant to build a roaster, not merely inform a reader.

The standard loop

  1. Read the lesson and write the decision it should improve.
  2. Plan one roast, observation, cupping, or brew test.
  3. Record what happened without decorating the result.
  4. Cup with the Cupping course method.
  5. Brew with a realistic Brewing course method.
  6. Choose one next change or explain why no change is justified.

Before advancing

  • You can explain the lesson in plain language.
  • You can name one error the lesson helps prevent.
  • You can connect the roast result to both cupping and brewing.
  • You have one written log entry.

After the core lessons

Use the Roasting Pathways guide to decide whether your next step is home roasting, garage or micro-roastery work, cafe roasting, or production roasting.
