How to use this

Read one lesson, apply one small practice, observe for a week, and record what changed. Scale only after the land answers back.

  • Built from KoffyKraft LRN and Natural Farming documents.
  • Cross-checked with soil health and agroecology references.
  • Designed for farmers, home growers, and field teams.
Core principles

The beginner map

Cover

Protect the soil from heat, hard rain, erosion, and moisture loss.

Roots

Keep living roots feeding the rhizosphere as much of the year as possible.

Less disturbance

Protect fine roots, fungal hyphae, worm channels, and soil aggregates.

Diversity

Use mixed plants and residues to support more functions and reduce fragility.
