Brewing 09

Espresso

Aim

Read espresso as a concentrated brew.

Start

Start near 18 g in, 36 g out, in 25-35 seconds.

Do

  • Pull a shot.
  • Record time and yield.
  • Taste before adjusting.

Notice

  • Sharp
  • bitter
  • thin
  • heavy

Words

Yield

Yield is the drink weight in the cup. If 18 g coffee gives 36 g espresso, the yield is 36 g.

Record

Question
Observation
Repeatable note
Next changeDo not chase numbers without tasting.
