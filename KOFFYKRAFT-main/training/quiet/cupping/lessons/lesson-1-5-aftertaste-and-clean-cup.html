Cupping 05

Aftertaste, clean cup

Aim

Notice what remains.

Start

Swallow one small sip, then wait 10-20 seconds.

Do

  • Sip.
  • Wait.
  • Write what stays.

Notice

  • Clean
  • muddy
  • dry
  • sweet

Words

Aftertaste

Aftertaste is what remains after swallowing or spitting. Clean means the finish is clear, not muddy or unpleasant.

Record

Question
Observation
Repeatable note
