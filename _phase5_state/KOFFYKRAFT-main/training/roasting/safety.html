Safety first

Safety and Setup for Roasting Self-Study

Roasting is not only a flavor exercise. It involves high heat, smoke, chaff, moving parts, electrical load, gas or power control, and fire risk.

Non-negotiables

  • Use a suitable roaster in a ventilated area.
  • Keep fire control equipment available and know how to use it.
  • Never leave a roast unattended.
  • Manage chaff, smoke, and hot surfaces.
  • Follow the machine manual and local safety rules.
  • Do not modify gas, electrical, or safety systems casually.

Learning boundary

This course can teach observation and decision-making. It cannot make an unsafe space safe. If the setup is questionable, fix the setup before roasting.
