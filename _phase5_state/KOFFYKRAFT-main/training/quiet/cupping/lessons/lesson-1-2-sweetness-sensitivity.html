Cupping 02

Sweetness

Aim

Find natural sweetness.

Start

Use two coffees if possible; taste from hot to cool.

Do

  • Taste slowly.
  • Let the cup cool.
  • Compare two cups.

Notice

  • Sugar-like
  • fruit-like
  • caramel-like

Words

Sweetness

Think of ripe fruit, jaggery, dates, honey, caramel, or sweet grain. Sweetness in coffee is usually a soft impression, not sugar.
